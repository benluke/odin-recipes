Pizza

Description

Pizza recipe with simple two ingredient crust

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups self-rising flour
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Other desired toppings

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Mix self rising flour and greek yogurt until forms a ball.
  3. Move ball to floured work surface and knead dough. if sticky, add more flour.
  4. Roll dough into a ball, cover in olive oil, spread dough out on pizza pan.
  5. Spread sauze, cheese, and any toppings on the crust.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes.
